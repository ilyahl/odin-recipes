Strawberry Salsa

Description

A sweet salsa that is great with pork and chicken dishes.

-Serves 2 people

Ingredients

  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 4 roma(plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. In large bowl, combine strawberries, tomatoes, chile peppers, garlic, lime juice and oil
  2. Toss together to mix and coat
  3. Cover dish and refrigerate for 2 hours to chill
  4. Ready to serve!
