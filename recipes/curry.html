Homemade South Indian Curry

Description

This is a simplified version of a curry my dad would cook. I have simplified the ingredients as much as possible, while maintaining that quintessential "South Indian Flare".

-Serves 2 people

Ingredients

  • Clove of garlic
  • Large white onion
  • 1 cup of uncooked basmati rice
  • Can of coconut milk
  • Can of Chickpeas
  • Tbsp of Oil

Steps

  1. Place pan on medium heat
  2. Add Oil to pan
  3. Dice onion
  4. Mince garlic
  5. Add minced garlic and sautee until brown
  6. Add minced onion and sautee until translucent
  7. Drain can of chickpeas, add to pan
  8. Add can of coconut milk
