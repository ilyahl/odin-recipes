Homemade South Indian Curry

Description

This is a simplified version of a curry my dad would cook. I have simplified the ingredients as much as possible, while maintaining that quintessential "South Indian Flare".

-Serves 2 people

Ingredients

  • Clove of garlic
  • Large white onion
  • 1 cup of uncooked basmati rice
  • Can of coconut milk
  • Can of Chickpeas
